Things to Know

~ Pinyin ~

Previous Next

Pinyin is the Romanization of the Chinese characters based on their pronunciation. In Mandarin Chinese, the phrase “Pin Yin” literally translates into “spell sound.” In the pictures , you can see two things, chinese and some english letters. The english words are the pinyin, and eaiser way to pronounce the chinese characters without having to understand the character. The pinyin has different tones(lines above some letters); it shows that the words are pronounced differently, and sometimes may even give it another meaning.

~ Character ~

There are two types of chinese characters. Simplified and Traditional. Simplified Chinese Characters are standardized chinese characters which are easier and more modern. Traditional Chinese Characters are currently used in some places; they are harder to write. Take a look at the pictures you can see some characters are harder to write than the other. It is simplified so it'll be eaiser write. However, while it may be eaiser to write, reading traditional characters are as easy as reading simplified characters.
